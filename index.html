ABOUT ME



Industrial Designer - Photographer.
Born in Orange County, proudly raised in Ixtapa Zihuatanejo, Mexico.
Studied in ITESM - Guadalajara, Mexico. (2010) Politecnico di Milano - Milan, Italy.

Published Photographer, Designer and Daydreamer with a Mission: Encourage people to appreciate the little things that surround us and often take for granted. I’ve always seen the world with a keen eye. Whether I’m traveling or just walking down the street, I like to stop for a second, observe and appreciate everything that is happening around me. Living in a fast paced world is making people focus on the superficial things and miss the little things in life that make happiness.
